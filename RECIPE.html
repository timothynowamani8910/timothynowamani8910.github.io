🇺🇬 Rolex - Ugandan Street Food

"Rolled Chapati with Eggs & Fresh Vegetables — The Taste of Kampala Streets!"
🥬 Ingredients
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (for chapatis)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil + extra for cooking
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 medium onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (diced)
  • 1 cup cabbage (shredded)
  • 1 carrot (grated)
  • Fresh green peppers (optional)
  • Salt & black pepper to taste

👩‍🍳 How to Make Rolex
  1. 1 Make chapati dough: Mix flour, salt, oil, and warm water. Knead for 5-7 minutes until smooth.
  2. 2 Rest dough for 30 minutes covered with a damp cloth.
  3. 3 Divide dough into small balls, roll each into thin circles.
  4. 4 Cook chapatis on hot pan until golden brown on both sides. Set aside.
  5. 5 Sauté onions, tomatoes, cabbage, and carrots in a pan until soft.
  6. 6 Whisk eggs with salt and pepper, pour over vegetables.
  7. 7 Cook egg-vegetable mixture like an omelette until set.
  8. 8 Place chapati flat, add egg mixture on top, roll tightly.
  9. 9 Wrap in paper or foil (traditional street style) and enjoy!
🇺🇬 Fun Fact: "Rolex" comes from "Rolled Eggs" — not the watch! It's Uganda's most popular street food, found on every corner in Kampala.
🍽️ Servings: 2
